Our Story

Building Dreams Since 1999

Skyline was founded with a single mission: to make finding, buying, or selling a property an effortless and enjoyable experience for every client.

Today, we operate across East Africa with a team of 150+ expert agents, managing over 12,000 premium property listings. Our technology-driven approach combined with personal service sets us apart in the market.
